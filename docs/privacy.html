Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Quickbrush is a client-side tool that respects your privacy. Since the tool is BYOK (Bring Your Own Key), your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.

Data We DON'T Collect
  • We do not collect your OpenAI API keys
  • We do not store your generated images on our servers
  • We do not track your usage patterns
  • We do not require accounts or authentication
Local Storage

Your OpenAI API key and generated images are stored locally in your browser using localStorage and IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device.
