A Message from Wispy Quickbrush

For years, I've traveled the land capturing the essence of heroes, villains, dragons, and the occasional confused goblin who wandered into my studio. But let's be honest— genius takes time, and not everyone can afford to commission a Wispy Quickbrush original (though you should, they're magnificent).

Don't tell the authorities.

What Is Quickbrush?

Quickbrush is your personal art studio in the cloud! Whether you're a Dungeon Master crafting the perfect scene, a player visualizing your character, or just someone who needs a picture of a disgruntled owlbear RIGHT NOW—we've got you covered.

Characters

Bring your heroes and villains to life! From noble paladins to questionable warlocks with suspiciously glowing eyes.

Scenes

Craft breathtaking environments! Taverns, dungeons, mystical forests, or that one cursed cave your party refuses to explore.

Creatures

Summon beasts, monsters, and things that go bump in the night! (Artist not responsible for nightmares.)

Items

Legendary swords, mysterious potions, cursed amulets, and that stick the bard insists is "actually really important."

How Does It Work?

Brushstrokes are our currency of creativity!

  • Low Quality (1 brushstroke) - Quick sketches, perfect for NPCs
  • Medium Quality (3 brushstrokes) - Detailed art for your adventures
  • High Quality (5 brushstrokes) - Museum-worthy masterpieces!

Simply describe what you want to see, choose your quality level, and let the magic—er, I mean, technology—do the rest! In moments, you'll have a unique piece of art worthy of any campaign.
